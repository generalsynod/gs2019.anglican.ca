Election of a new Primate – Results

On Saturday, July 13, the 42nd General Synod of the Anglican Church of Canada elected The Rt. Rev. Dr. Linda Nicholls of the Diocese of Huron has been elected the 14th Primate of the Anglican Church of Canada.

Primate-elect Nicholls will be installed as the 14th Primate of the Anglican Church of Canada on Tuesday, July 16 at Christ Church Cathedral in Vancouver, British Columbia.
